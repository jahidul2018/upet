About Us

  • Help dogs /cats with sterilization / fostering /adoption / vaccination.

  • Giving general idea about medical support and veterinary help.

  • WE DON”T SELL or BUY ANIMALS HERE.WE ARE RESCUERS

upet was created mainly for the stray animals of Bangladesh. we rescue, foster and give them up for adoption. we try to educate pet owners about urgent veterinary help, vaccinations and sterilizations. we use our group as a platform for helping and saving animals.
